1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the owners of the site. For our UUID Generator service, cookies help us deliver a better and more personalized experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

UUID Generator uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are essential for using features like our bulk generation tool.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies enable the website to remember choices you make (such as dark mode preference) to provide enhanced, more personal features.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site, including features like the UUID decoder.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners (e.g., Monetag) to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements.

3. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics and deliver advertisements on our site, including:

  • Google Analytics
  • Monetag

These third parties may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect or receive information from our website and other places on the internet. For more information about how these companies use cookies, you can visit the About Cookies resource site.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser
  • Allow site-specific cookies
  • Block third-party cookies

To find out more about how to manage cookies in your web browser, visit:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie banner allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking on the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website or by managing your browser settings as described above.

6. Changes to Our Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and the "Last updated" date modified accordingly. We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically for any changes. For more significant changes, we may notify you through other means, such as by emailing you or by placing a prominent notice on our website.

7. Cookie Use and Our Services

Our services, including the API and developer resources, function best with cookies enabled. However, you can still use most features of our site with cookies disabled or blocked.
