1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files saved by websites. They help things work smoothly and remember your choices. For our UUID Generator, they make the experience nicer and keep preferences like dark mode.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Site basics and features like bulk generation.
  • Preferences: Your choices (e.g., dark mode).
  • Analytics: Understand performance and improve features like the UUID decoder.
  • Advertising: Partners like Monetag may set cookies to show relevant ads.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We also use third-party cookies for stats and ads, such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Monetag

They may use cookies and similar tech across sites. Learn more at About Cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser
  • Allow site-specific cookies
  • Block third-party cookies

To learn more, see:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

5. Cookie Consent

On first visit, you’ll see a banner to accept or decline non‑essential cookies. You can change choices anytime via “Cookie Settings” in the footer.

6. Changes to Our Cookie Policy

We may update this page. The date above will change when we do.

7. Cookie Use and Our Services

Our API and developer resources work best with cookies on, but most of the site still works without them.
