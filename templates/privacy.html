1. Intro

We respect your privacy. This page explains what we collect and how we use it when you use easyuuidgenerator.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our UUID Generator, we may collect:

  • Usage data: IP address, browser, pages visited, time on page, device info.
  • API usage: Request counts and rate-limit info for the API.
  • Cookies: Used to remember preferences (e.g., dark mode) and for analytics/ads. See the Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use this data to:

  • Run and improve the UUID tools (including bulk and decoder).
  • Monitor usage and keep the service reliable.
  • Fix bugs and prevent abuse.
  • Understand site performance and UX.
  • Show ads via Monetag where enabled.

4. Data Retention

We keep usage logs for up to 30 days. We don’t keep more than needed.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We use trusted providers to help run the site, like:

  • Analytics: To understand website usage.
  • Advertising: Monetag, to show relevant ads.

We expect partners to follow strong privacy practices.

6. Advertising

We use Monetag for ads. See Monetag’s privacy policy for details.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate information
  • The right to erasure of your personal data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability

Local laws vary (e.g., GDPR). We’ll do our best to help with requests.
