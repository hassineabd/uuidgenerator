1. Introduction

UUID Generator ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at easyuuidgenerator.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our UUID Generator service, we may collect:

  • Usage Data: Information on how you access and use our service, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and unique device identifiers.
  • API Usage: When using our API, we collect API calls, rate limits, and usage patterns to improve our service and enforce fair usage.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our UUID generation service, including the bulk generator and UUID decoder
  • Monitoring the usage of our service
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues
  • Improving our website and user experience
  • Displaying relevant advertisements through our partner Monetag

4. Data Retention

We retain usage logs for a maximum of 30 days. We do not store any personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary to provide our service.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our service ("Service Providers"), including:

  • Analytics providers: To help us understand website usage
  • Advertising partners: Including Monetag, to display relevant advertisements

We use industry standard practices to ensure these service providers meet high privacy standards. For more information about how third-party providers handle data, you can refer to resources like the Privacy Shield Framework.

6. Advertising

We use Monetag as our advertising partner. Monetag may collect and use data for targeted advertising. For more information about how Monetag processes your data, please refer to their privacy policy.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate information
  • The right to erasure of your personal data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing

For more information about data protection rights in different regions, you can visit resources like the EU's GDPR information portal or the Australian Privacy Principles.
